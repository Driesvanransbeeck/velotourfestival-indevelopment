VeloTour Festival

Discover where bike touring can take you.
From 1 to 3 May 2020 around Brussels.

  • Register now
Learn More

The first Velo Tour Festival will take place around Brussels from Friday 1 to Sunday 3 May 2020!

100 cycling enthusiasts from all over Belgium will gather to make a 3-day bicycle tour in Belgium, share their excitement about bike touring and have a great time together. This festival is not-for-profit and entirely organised by a group of volunteers.

DAY 1: From Brussels to Flanders

Meeting at Muntpunt in Brussels. After a briefing and OpenStreetMap workshop, we'll start a 50 km bicycle ride to Flemish Brabant. At night, we get together to have dinner and share stories, ideas and inspiration. Camping in a field in the countryside.

DAY 2: From Flanders to Wallonia

Start with a healthy breakfast. 60 km through Pajottenland to reach Wallonia. Dinner followed by movie night under the stars! And yes, we'll sleep again in our tents.

DAY 3: From Wallonia to Brussels

Last breakfast together before final ride of 60 km to go back to Brussels. Farewell drink to say goodbye and see you next year!

All you need to know

Clear expectations make good friends. We want you to read this FAQ section throroughly so that you know what you're getting yourself into.

  • Why this Festival

    Exploring by bicycle makes you feel alive. It's a unique way to discover a country. What if you combine this with meeting other enthusiasts and have a great time together?

    The VeloTour Festival gives you the opportunity to travel by bicycle in Belgium and meet new people.

  • For whom?

    For both curious-minded people who want to discover bicycle touring and experienced bicycle travellers to share their passion and stories.

    From solo travelers to families, everyone is welcome. If you are travelling with your 10 best friends, we doubt you’ll still have time to meet others, so no groups please!

  • Equipment

    Come with your bicycle. You'll carry your own panniers with camping gear (tent, sleeping bag & mattress). Yet, not owning equipment shouldn't stop you for participating, ask a friend or leave a comment in the registration form.

  • Participation fee

    40 euro per person (20 euro per kid). It includes 2x breakfast, 2x dinner, insurance, social activities, dry toilets & overhead. Lunch & drinks are not included. Full budget here.

  • Itinerary

    3 days of cycling 50 to 60 km. We won't cycle together as 1 big group. You'll receive all details on the first day during the briefing.

  • Activities

    In the evening there will be social activities around bicycle touring (film projection, travel presentation or even a concert empowered by bycicles). Those activities won't be next to the tents, so that you can rest when if you want to.

  • Organisation

    The festival is a not-for-profit project organised by volunteers. During the festival all partipants are expected to give a hand in either the morning or evening.

  • Insurance

    There's a liability and physical damage insurance during the event. Yet, you participate at your own risk and responsibility, and will be asked to sign a disclaimer on the first day. You must respect the highway code at any time.

  • Code of conduct

    The goal of the VeloTour Festival is to meet new people and get to know each other. It's a must to be open towards others and to agree with our code of conduct.

  • Languages

    The VeloTour Festival is organised à la belge: a good mix of English, French and Dutch. Whatever language you speak, we'll always find a way to communicate with another.
