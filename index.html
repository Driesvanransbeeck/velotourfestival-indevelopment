The first Velo Tour Festival will take place from Friday 1 to Sunday 3 May 2020!

100 cycling enthusiasts from all over Belgium will gather to make a 3-day bicycle tour in Belgium, share their excitement about bike touring and have a great time together. This festival is not-for-profit and entirely organised by a group of volunteers.
